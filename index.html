Hangman Game

Object of the game and rules:

The object of the game is to not die by correctly guessing a word one letter at a time. The player will pick a letter and if the word contains the letter guessed, it will appear. If the letter guessed is incorrect, the player is one guess closer to being hanged. Play will continue until the full word is revealed or has guessed incorrectly 6 times, whichever is first.
